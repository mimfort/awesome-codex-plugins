# Local Sponsorship Link Building Examples

Examples for prospecting, evaluation, fulfillment, and relationship add-ons.

## Sponsorship Prospecting Queries

Use city, county, neighborhood, or ZIP modifiers.

- `intitle:"Our sponsors" [city]`
- `"Our sponsors" [city]`
- `"thank you to our generous sponsors" [city]`
- `"our donors" [city]`
- `"our partners" [city]`
- `[city] [category] sponsorship`
- `[city] [audience] event sponsors`

Add category modifiers such as families, arts, health, pets, education, sports, festivals, schools, PTAs, or industry associations.

## Sponsorship Evaluation Rubric

| Criterion | Strong Signal |
|-----------|---------------|
| Goal fit | Benefits map directly to the primary campaign goal |
| Local relevance | Audience is truly in the target city or neighborhood |
| Audience fit | Attendees, subscribers, donors, or readers overlap with target customers |
| Link value | Sponsor page, article, or blog opportunity can link to a useful local URL |
| Reach | Credible local attendance, email, social, web, print, or press reach |
| Mission fit | Natural brand, community, employee, or audience connection |
| Timing | Approval, payment, and fulfillment can happen in time |
| Reliability | Responsive organizer and written confirmation |
| Cost efficiency | Benefits compare well with comparable local channels |
| Relationship upside | Future content, PR, employee, or community opportunities |

## Asset Packet Checklist

- Logo for light and dark backgrounds.
- Square logo variant.
- Correct destination URL and UTM URL.
- One-sentence company description.
- Coupon code if available.
- Social, newsletter, blog, or interview copy if included.
- Print-ready ads or files when needed.

## Existing Relationship Ideas

- Testimonial.
- High-quality local or product images.
- Preferred vendor badge.
- Sponsorship badge or sponsor profile.
- Interview or dialogue.
- Unlinked mention conversion.
- Local-focused content.
- Press release for local news or blog contacts.
