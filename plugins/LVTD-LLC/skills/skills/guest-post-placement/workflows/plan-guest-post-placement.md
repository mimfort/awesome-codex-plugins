# Plan Guest Post Placement Workflow

Use this workflow to plan a guest post placement campaign.

## When to Use

- The user wants guest post links or placements.
- A sales or product page needs citability.
- Guest post opportunity quality or viability needs review.

## Prerequisites

- Target page and business goal.
- Target keywords or audience.
- Available SMEs, authors, and approval path.
- Risk constraints.

**Reference**: `references/core/knowledge.md`, `references/core/rules.md`

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Define Goal And Target

- [ ] Record target page, business goal, target keywords, expertise signal, and risk constraints.
- [ ] Identify desired link target and acceptable citation context.

### Step 2: Audit Citability

- [ ] Identify existing citable elements.
- [ ] Recommend additions if the page is too commercial.
- [ ] Anchor citations to specific sections when possible.

### Step 3: Name The Audience

- [ ] Translate keywords into audience/category names.
- [ ] Map practitioner tasks, skill level, setting, constraints, and problems.
- [ ] Use audience names for prospecting and titles.

### Step 4: Check Viability And Quality

- [ ] Run audience plus guest-post footprint queries.
- [ ] Count definite opportunities in the first result window.
- [ ] Score editorial quality, reach, community, backlinks, outbound behavior, and anchor patterns.

### Step 5: Build Content And Outreach Plan

- [ ] Create two or three title ideas per segment.
- [ ] Match each title to audience benefit and citation target.
- [ ] Confirm SME inputs and approvals.
- [ ] Prepare tracking sheets before outreach.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It's Bad | Do Instead |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| Keyword without audience | Produces generic pitches | Name practitioner audience |
| Commercial link target | Editors reject or remove it | Add citable elements |
| Finished article in first email | Overcommits content | Pitch titles first |
| No content tracking | Causes duplicates | Track status and retire placed content |

## Exit Criteria

Task is complete when:

- [ ] Target page has a natural citation reason or required changes are listed.
- [ ] Audience names and title ideas are defined.
- [ ] Prospect quality rubric and tracking fields are ready.
- [ ] Outreach can hand off to `link-outreach-acquisition`.
