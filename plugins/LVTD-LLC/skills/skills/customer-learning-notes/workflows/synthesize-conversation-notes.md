# Synthesize Conversation Notes

Use this workflow after one or more customer conversations.

## Steps

- [ ] Identify the segment and conversation context for each note set.
- [ ] Separate direct evidence from the team's interpretation.
- [ ] Tag pains, goals, workarounds, money, people, features, emotions, and
  follow-ups.
- [ ] Group notes by recurring problem, current behavior, and buying path.
- [ ] Flag evidence that may come from a different segment.
- [ ] Mark contradictions and likely bad data.
- [ ] Update the team's beliefs and open questions.
- [ ] Recommend the next action: more conversations, narrower segment, concrete
  commitment ask, prototype, traction test, or stop.

## Evidence Rules

- Prefer specific past behavior over opinions.
- Preserve exact wording only when useful for alignment or positioning.
- Link feature requests to the motivation behind them.
- Treat strong emotion as a signal to dig, not as proof by itself.
- Label confidence as high, medium, or low using the parent skill's confidence
  rules.

## Output

Return an evidence table, belief updates, decisions, contradictions, and the
next three questions.

## Exit Criteria

The synthesis produces belief updates, decisions, and the next three questions.
