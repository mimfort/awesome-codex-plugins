# Experience Brief Workflow

## Procedure

1. State the target player in behavioral terms.
2. Write the experience promise.
3. Identify 3-5 primary motivations.
4. Identify likely friction:
   - learning burden
   - control burden
   - reading burden
   - difficulty spikes
   - unclear goals
   - slow payoff
5. Draft the first-session interest curve.
6. Convert experience goals into build requirements.
7. Define telemetry and playtest observations.

```markdown

## Experience Promise

## Motivation Map
```
