# Link Prospect Qualification Examples

Examples for scoring and segmenting prospects.

## Prospect Decisions

| Prospect | Decision | Reason |
|----------|----------|--------|
| University library resource page about the exact topic | Priority | Strong relevance, user-serving intent, trusted curator, clear resource fit |
| High-DA site with "write for us" and many unrelated outbound links | Reject or review | Authority is not enough; paid/link-farm signals need investigation |
| Active niche blog that covered a similar resource last year | Standard or priority | Editorial fit and contextual pitch angle are clear |
| Old directory with no editorial standards | Low priority or reject | Easy link but weak trust and copyable value |
| Local nonprofit sponsor page for a local business | Standard | Local relevance and community intent may outweigh lower authority |

## Reject Reason Examples

- No audience fit with target asset.
- Link-sale language or sponsored-post footprint.
- Competitor with no partnership rationale.
- Page abandoned and no contact path.
- Asset is not ready: thin, orphaned, or sales-only.

## Qualification Note Example

"Keep as standard outreach. The page is a maintained resource list for teachers, links to comparable guides, and the target asset has a student worksheet that fits the audience. Score is limited by low visible reach and unclear contact owner."
