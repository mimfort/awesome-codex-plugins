# Link Outreach Acquisition Knowledge

Core concepts for relationship-led and audience-relevant link outreach.

## Key Concepts

### Link Acquisition

Outreach should earn mentions by showing why the linked asset helps the publisher's audience. The request may be a useful share, citation, resource update, story tip, contribution, attribution fix, or replacement suggestion.

### Relationship Warming

Before asking, look for genuine ways to help: cite them, share their work, include them in expert content, promote to a newsletter or social audience, or offer a useful introduction.

### Contextual Relevance

Personalization must connect the prospect's actual work, audience, or page context to the specific asset. A generic compliment with a merged name is worse than a clean template.

### Audience Benefit

Every pitch should answer: why would this publisher's readers, visitors, followers, or editors care?

### Submission Acquisition

Some opportunities are one-touch submissions such as event listings, niche directories, resource pages, PDF repositories, or forms. Capture them, but do not let copyable submissions replace higher-trust relationship outreach.
